Water Park Attractions

Water Park

As a member of the World Water Park Association, USA. the Fun N Food water park offers India's largest collection of Water slides and attractions for the toddler learning how to swim to the Water sports enthusiast seeking a thrill. Thrills' chills and spills don't miss them at the Fun n Food water park offers its patrons a new twisting turning and sliding experience with the longest Water slide in the Country.

Action River

For those looking for a gentler experience, there is the Lazy river which is over 800 feet long with a gentle flow and a refreshment counter.

Wave Pool

A big attraction with the young and old alike is the Wave Pool- an artificial sea where huge waves are generated in three different patterns.If you have had enough, relax by the wave pool under thatched roof umbrellas and sip on a cool mock tail.

Water Slides

Chills, Thrills and Spills with a combination of Tube slides, body slides and flumes offers some thing for every one.

The Family Slide

An exciting tube slide in which a family of 4 can ride together to splash into a jaccuzi

The Tornado

A cyclonic experience as the rider shoots through the Typhoon tunnel into the sensation of a whirl pool in the tornado bowl before splashing into the water

Speed Coaster

The thrill of a roller coaster, and the fun of water as the rider follows a pendulum motion.

Aqua Shute

Looking to test your nerves, try the Aqua Shute, the fastest slide in Asia where one can sit on a sledge and splash into the pool at great speeds

New Water Park

New water park area with Multi racer slide, Family slide, Twister slide, Animal showers, Rain bow showers, Water Guns, Bucket showers, Water Guns, Kiddie slides

The Twister

An illusion, an experience as the rider slides through intertwined tunnels.

Multi Racer

Multi Lane mat type racer. Riders race down side by side on this multi lane racer.

Kiddie Pools

For Kids in the age group of 1-5 years, there is a Kids Pool consisting of a Frog Slide and different types of toys and showers where the Kids can ride and float. Shallow Pool with Mushroom showers, Bucket showers, Water Guns and Kiddie slides

Rain Dance

To complete the Water thrill , it is the Rain dance pool to forget the world and let the rhythm take over.The entire Water park area is covered with synthetic grass which not only enhances the beauty of the Park but ensures the safety of visitors who might slip due to wet feet.
